Our Products

Sesame Seeds

A small oil-rich seed from Sesamum indicum, commonly used for oil production and as a food ingredient. High in healthy fats, protein, calcium and antioxidants.

Hibiscus

Dried petals of Hibiscus sabdariffa used to make a popular sour drink. Contains vitamin C and antioxidants known for refreshing taste and health benefits.

Soybeans

A protein-rich legume widely used in food products like soy milk, tofu and oil. Contains essential amino acids making it a good plant-based protein source.

Ginger

A spicy root used as a spice and medicinal plant. Helps with digestion, reduces inflammation and is commonly used in teas and cooking.

Groundnut

A legume grown for its edible seeds. Rich in protein, healthy fats and vitamins. Used to make peanut oil, butter and snacks.

Tigernut

A nutritious tuber rich in fiber, healthy fats and natural sugars. Popular for making tigernut milk and used as a healthy snack.

Maize

A staple grain crop widely used for food, animal feed and industrial purposes. Available in large quantities for export.
